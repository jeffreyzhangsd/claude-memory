For side-hustle / monetization work, default to paths that DO NOT require building a public persona or audience.

**Why:** During monetization brainstorm 2026-05-19, user explicitly rejected newsletter/Discord/Patreon ideas because "I really don't think I'm that big into [niche] and can reach an audience when there's so many larger creators." Recognizes the customer-acquisition tradeoff ("to make money you need customers but yeah") but wants to minimize ongoing presence-building burden. Also flagged not wanting to fake expertise in domains they don't actually understand (e.g. biotech tracker rejected on grounds of "I don't really understand that").

**How to apply:** When suggesting income opportunities, default to:

1. **Platform/marketplace products** — Notion templates, Etsy digital downloads, Gumroad/Lemon Squeezy products, Chrome Web Store extensions, Mac/iOS app stores, Shopify app store, Unity/Fab asset store. Platform brings customers; user stays invisible.
2. **Capital plays** — wheel strategy, options, sports models, crypto. No customers required. Capital risk is the cost.
3. **Cold B2B outbound** — local lead-gen, cold email to trade businesses. Hustle but not persona.

Skip by default (unless user re-asks): newsletter pitches, Discord community growth, Substack, X/Twitter audience-build, "post on Reddit to drive signups." Also avoid pitching niches where user has no genuine domain knowledge — they will reject on plausibility grounds even if the numbers are good.
